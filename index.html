• Who We Serve

    We connect providers, payers and innovators, working alongside them to create an ecosystem of care that spans across in-person, virtual and automated care.

    • Providers We create a connected ecosystem of care that improves the clinician experience and provides better patient access throughout the care continuum.
    • Payers We integrate your ecosystem of digital care services into a single experience to centralizing care through your digital front door.
    • Government We equip government agencies to increase readiness, expand access, lower costs, and improve outcomes for those that serve.
    • Higher Education We offer personalized digital health programs to provide students and faculty with the right amount of care and support when they need it.
    • Private Practices We enable independent providers and group practices to deliver seamless access to secure, HIPAA compliant digital care.

  • What We Offer

    It starts with our future-ready platform—a convergence of technologies, services and devices that enables care delivery at scale, anytime and any place.

    • Provider Solutions Our comprehensive platform connects in-person, virtual, and automated touchpoints, providing you with a digitally-enabled care model that extends across the entire patient journey.
    • Payer Solutions Our innovative platform seamlessly integrates into your existing infrastructure, empowering you to lower costs, improve outcomes and enhance the member experience.
    • Digital Care Platform Our digital care enablement platform is purpose-built to seamlessly integrate in-person, virtual and automated care – providing a complete, connected and intelligent longitudinal healthcare experience.
    • Specialty Consults Shrava Specialty Consults enables your clinicians to connect with remote providers and patients at any time using purpose-built carts, TV kits, tablets, and peripheral devices.
    • Provider Network Services Our two national clinician networks, Shrava Medical Group® and Shrava Psychiatric Care™, can serve as an extension of your provider networks.
